Biography

Tanatswa Madhoyo is an upcoming Zimbabwean gospel musician known for heartfelt and spiritually uplifting music. She developed her passion through church performances and continues to grow as a powerful voice in gospel music, under the guidance of her parents, Pastors Simon and Nyasha Madhoyo.
